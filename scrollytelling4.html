Prohibited Contributions

×

Candidates voluntarily using the program cannot accept money from:

  • Political Action Committees (PACs)
  • Corporations
  • Labor Unions
  • LLCs
  • State or local central committees of a political party
Individual donor cap
No single person may contribute more than $500 to a candidate over the course of a campaign.

Small money, multiplied, wins.

Under a 7× match, ten $10 donors send $800 to a candidate — more than a single $100 donor's $650.
