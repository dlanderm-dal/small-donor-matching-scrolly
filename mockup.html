Once a publicly financed candidate for County Executive receives a combined $40,000 from at least 500 residents of their jurisdiction, the matching kicks in. The county only matches donors from the candidate’s jurisdiction and it’s cumulative based on donor meaning it doesn’t matter if someone gives their donation all at once or in smaller pieces.
For example: one $100 donation is treated the same as if the same person donated $50 twice.
